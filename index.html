While I found the foundations of many programs like HTML and CSS frustrating, once I understood the bone structures, I found coding to be very enjoyable. I started by being able to design a website layout from scratch changing colors, fonts, functions, etc., and have moved quickly into other programs. Coding can be very creative!

During my junior year e-learning experience, I took web design classes where I learned how to use additional programs and I really took to Photoshop. I worked on many introductory class projects and then found online tutorials which taught me many new tools embedded in Photoshop. I really enjoyed studying these and used many new creative skills for assigned school work. My creative journey in technology is just getting going and I want to be able to expand my skills further. What I have learned about myself during my four years in high school is that I am very dependable. lending a hand is always a generous thing to do. He never gives up when the going gets tough and I respect him for that. In my senior year of high school, I have taken a business class, so I have a general idea of different techniques I can use to promote different kinds of businesses, but at the same time, I want to be able to go to college and be able to learn more about business.
